01 // ABOUT

Who I Am

I'm Animesh Sitoula, a Class 11 student from Nepal with a deep passion for technology, cybersecurity, and programming. I'm fascinated by how computer systems operate under the hood — from network packets to kernel-level processes.

My goal is to become a skilled ethical hacker and cybersecurity professional. I'm currently building that foundation by mastering Python, networking fundamentals, Linux systems, and introductory security tools like those found in Kali Linux.

I believe in learning by doing. Rather than passively studying theory, I apply every concept I learn to a real project — whether that's a data analysis tool, a network scanner, or a security lab environment.

This portfolio is my public learning log — a showcase of the projects, experiments, and skills I've built on the way to becoming a cybersecurity professional.
